Mechanical Engineering Graduate & Design Lead

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a mechanical engineer focused on leveraging finite element analysis (FEA), computational modeling, and rigorous empirical validation to solve complex design challenges. My experience spans full-lifecycle engineering integration, from structural optimizations to thermal analysis.

I thrive in cross-functional team environments where complex engineering requirements meet strict manufacturing realities. Whether optimizing cell matrices or testing fluid dynamics, I aim to design robust solutions backed by data.

Technical Focus

My coursework and hands-on projects are deeply rooted in solid mechanics, structural dynamics, and heat transfer. I frequently work with tools like SolidWorks Simulation and MATLAB to run transient models and analyze boundary conditions.

I am highly interested in advanced manufacturing, structural design optimization, and prototyping within the energy and automotive sectors.

Team Leadership

Engineering is a team sport. Beyond running simulations, I place a high premium on clear coordination and documentation. I have served as a communications lead across multi-disciplinary teams—bridging the gap between engineering designers, external suppliers, and clients.

Managing deadlines, manufacturing constraints, and dynamic design variations has shaped my engineering approach just as much as formulas and CAD layouts.

Beyond Engineering
