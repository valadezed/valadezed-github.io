Engineering Capstone

System Integration & Structural Mounting

Utilizing SolidWorks and Finite Element Analysis (FEA) to design and validate high-integrity mounting solutions against dynamic loading environments.

Role Communications & Design Lead
Timeline Senior Capstone
Tools Used SolidWorks, FEA, MATLAB
Core Focus Structural Integrity

1. The Design Challenge

The primary objective of this integration project was engineering a robust mounting subsystem capable of isolating critical components from severe multi-axis vibration profiles and dynamic forces during operation.

Project Constraint: The assembly had to maintain a high factor of safety ($FS \ge 1.5$) while minimizing total structural weight to satisfy rigid customer load parameters.

2. FEA Studies & Simulation

Using SolidWorks Simulation, the team constructed rigorous finite element models to predict localized stress concentrations and structural displacement under peak fatigue parameters.
